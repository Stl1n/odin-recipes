Pizzatoast

Description

A Pizza toast is an easy to make dish.

It is both, a strange Pizza and a strange toast.

Ingredients for one toast

  • One toast
  • tomato paste
  • Everything you would put on a Pizza

Steps

  1. Toast the toast
  2. Spread the tomato paste on the toast
  3. Put everything else on it
  4. Put it into the oven at around 200 degrees Celsius for about 7 Minutes
