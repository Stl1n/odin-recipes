A simple Burger

Description

A Burger is a very popular food, why don't you make one yourself?

Ingredients for one Burger

  • One burger pattie
  • A Burger bun
  • Salat
  • Tomatos
  • Onions
  • Sauces
  • Everything else you would like to find on your Burger

Steps

  1. Put your burger pattie into a pan and fry it
  2. When the burger pattie is nearly done cut the bun into two halfes and toast it in the pan using some butter
  3. Take everything out of the pan
  4. Build your Burger
